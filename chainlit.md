# Welcome to RAWV

RAWV is your voice-first research agent.

## What RAWV Does

- Understands voice and text input
- Runs transparent web research when needed
- Shows reasoning steps and sources
- Speaks responses back with natural voice output

## Quick Tips

- Ask casual things like "hello" for normal chat
- Ask factual questions or use "research" phrasing to trigger web research
- Use the visible research controls to force research on or off
